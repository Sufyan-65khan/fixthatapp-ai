What Makes a Strong Password?

A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid common words, names, or patterns.

Tips for Password Security

Use a unique password for every account. Consider using a password manager to keep track of them. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
