Organic Rankings Dropped: Technical and Content Recovery Plan

When rankings decline sharply, isolate whether the cause is technical, algorithmic, or competitive.

1. Verify Tracking and Coverage

  • Confirm analytics and Search Console are collecting data correctly.
  • Compare affected URLs in Search Console Performance and Pages reports.

2. Audit Recent Site Changes

  • Template updates, redirects, canonicals, robots rules, and internal linking changes.
  • Page speed regressions and Core Web Vitals degradation.
  • Content pruning or URL removals that impacted topical depth.

3. Evaluate Content and Intent Match

  • Refresh outdated content and add missing entities/subtopics.
  • Improve E-E-A-T signals: author info, citations, and first-hand evidence where possible.
  • Re-check search intent alignment against current top results.

4. Recovery Actions

  1. Fix technical blockers first (indexability, redirects, crawl issues).
  2. Prioritize high-impression pages for content updates.
  3. Strengthen internal links to strategic pages.
  4. Monitor weekly trend changes before making additional large edits.
