About FixThatApp

Free tools and practical guides — built for real people with real problems.

FixThatApp is an independent utility website launched in 2025. It was built out of a simple frustration: when something breaks — an app, a calculator, a tool you rely on — finding a clear, no-nonsense answer is harder than it should be. Most results are either too vague, behind a paywall, or written by someone who has clearly never experienced the problem themselves.

FixThatApp was created to be different: every guide, every calculator, every tool is designed to get you to a working answer in under two minutes. No accounts. No subscription. No "upgrade to premium." Just working solutions.

What We Publish

FixThatApp covers three main areas:

  • Free online tools — calculators (GST, SIP, compound interest, loan EMI, tax, profit margin), text utilities (duplicate remover, line sorter, whitespace cleaner, diff checker), developer tools (JWT decoder, hash generator, URL encoder, JSON formatter), and productivity tools (Pomodoro timer, date calculator, timezone planner).
  • Troubleshooting guides — step-by-step fixes for common problems with apps like WhatsApp, Discord, Netflix, Spotify, Instagram, Gmail, and hundreds more. Each guide walks through likely causes and solutions in order of probability, not in order of what's easiest to write.
  • Tool and reference guides — in-depth explainers for how calculators and utilities work. Not just "use the tool" — but why the math works, what the formulas mean, what common mistakes to avoid, and when you'd use one approach vs another.

Our Editorial Standards

  • No generic advice. Every guide is written for a specific problem, not adapted from a generic template. "Try restarting your device" is not a guide; it's a guess. We explain what causes the problem and why each step fixes it.
  • No paywalls. Every tool and guide is free to use without creating an account. The site is supported by display advertising (Google AdSense), which is clearly disclosed in our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.
  • Accurate calculations. All calculators (GST, tax, SIP, compound interest, loan EMI, profit margin, etc.) use standard financial formulas. Complex calculations reference official methodology — for example, India's GST slab structure follows official government schedules.
  • Content is updated. Troubleshooting guides are reviewed when platform behavior changes. Blog articles are revised when formulas, regulations, or best practices evolve. The "Updated" date on each page reflects the last substantive review.
  • Privacy is respected. All tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the inputs you enter into any calculator or text tool. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
  • Transparent advertising. We display Google AdSense ads. Ads are clearly separate from editorial content. We do not accept sponsored articles or paid-for guide placements.

Why Build This?

There are thousands of calculator websites. Most are designed to rank in search results and show ads — not to actually help users understand what they're calculating. A GST calculator that gives you a number but doesn't explain inclusive vs exclusive pricing, or what the reverse calculation means, has half-done its job. FixThatApp tries to give both: the instant answer from the tool, and the explanation that helps you understand and trust the result.

The same principle applies to troubleshooting: a guide that lists 12 possible fixes without explaining why any of them work leaves users guessing. FixThatApp guides explain the likely cause, why it happens, and which fix addresses which cause — so you can skip straight to the one that applies to your situation.
