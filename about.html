About FixThatApp

FixThatApp is an independent troubleshooting website focused on practical fixes for common app, device, and web service problems.

What We Publish

  • Step-by-step troubleshooting guides for real user issues.
  • Tool tutorials for developers and everyday users.
  • SEO and technical troubleshooting resources for website owners.

How Content Is Created

Our content is written and reviewed for clarity, usability, and relevance. We update pages when platforms change behavior or new fixes become available.

Editorial Principles

  • Clear instructions over vague advice.
  • No paywalled fixes.
  • Transparent use of third-party advertising.
