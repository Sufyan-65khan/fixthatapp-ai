Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FixThatApp ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use www.fixthatapp.com. We have written it to be readable — not as a legal wall of text designed to obscure what actually happens.

What We Collect Directly

Contact form submissions. When you use the contact form on our Contact page, we receive your name, email address, selected topic, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add contact form submissions to any mailing list, and we do not share them with third parties.

Tool inputs. All calculators and text tools on FixThatApp run entirely in your browser. The values you enter (loan amounts, text, passwords, JSON, etc.) are processed locally — they are never transmitted to our servers. We have no record of what you calculated or converted.

No accounts, no tracking pixels, no newsletter signups. FixThatApp has no user accounts. We do not run any first-party analytics. We do not place tracking pixels from Facebook, Twitter, or any other social platform.

What Third-Party Services Collect Automatically

When you visit FixThatApp, third-party services integrated into the site may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the site you came from)
  • Device type and screen resolution

These data points are collected by the services described below, not by FixThatApp directly.

Advertising — Google AdSense

FixThatApp uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites across the internet. This is how the site is funded — there are no paywalls or subscriptions.

We implement cookie consent on this site. If you click "Reject" on the cookie banner, we instruct AdSense to serve non-personalised ads only, meaning your browsing history is not used for ad targeting.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

Cookies

FixThatApp uses the following cookies:

  • fta_cookie_consent — a first-party cookie that stores your cookie preference (accepted or rejected). Expires after one year.
  • Google AdSense cookies — set by Google to serve and measure ads. These are third-party cookies subject to Google's own privacy policy.

No other cookies are set by FixThatApp.

Third-Party Links

Troubleshooting guides frequently link to official support pages, app stores, and status pages. These are external sites with their own privacy practices. We link to them for reference — we are not responsible for their content or data handling.

Children

FixThatApp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our contact form, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

If you have submitted a message through our contact form and would like it deleted, email us at contact@fixthatapp.com with the subject "Data deletion request." We will confirm deletion within 5 business days.

Changes to This Policy

We update this policy when our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. For minor wording corrections, we update the date. For substantive changes, we add a note describing what changed.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact form or contact@fixthatapp.com.
