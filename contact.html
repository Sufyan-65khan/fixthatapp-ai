What to Include

  • The URL of the page with the issue
  • What you expected to happen and what actually happened
  • Your browser and device type (e.g. Chrome on Android) if reporting a tool bug
  • A screenshot if it helps explain the problem

We do not provide one-on-one technical support for third-party apps (WhatsApp, Netflix, etc.). Our troubleshooting guides cover the most common fixes — if your issue isn't resolved by the guide, the next step is usually contacting that app's official support team.
