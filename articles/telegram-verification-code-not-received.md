Title: Troubleshooting Guide for Telegram Verification Code Not Received

Problem Description:
Telegram verification codes are sent to your registered mobile phone number. However, you're not receiving them or are experiencing difficulties in receiving them.

Possible Causes:
1. Telegram Settings: Ensure that you have enabled the "Less secure apps" setting in your phone's settings. This setting requires your phone to be online for the codes to be sent.

2. Telegram App: Some users have reported that if they have not connected their phone to the internet when they tried to send the verification code, it wouldn't work.

3. Network Issues: Your phone might not be connected to the internet. Try reconnecting your phone and checking if the verification code is sent.

4. Wrong Number: If you have entered the wrong number or the code isn't meant for that number, try entering a different number.

Step-by-step fixes:

1. Enable Less secure apps: Go to your phone's settings and turn on the "Less secure apps" setting.

2. Reconnect your phone: If the verification code isn't sent, try reconnecting your phone to the internet.

3. Check your phone: Make sure your phone is online and try to send the verification code again.

4. Enter a different number: If you entered the wrong number, try entering a different number and see if the verification code is sent to that number.

When to contact support:
If you're still experiencing issues or have not received a verification code, consider contacting our customer support team. They can provide you with the most accurate and effective solutions for your problem.

FAQ:
Q: How can I verify my phone number?
A: Use the code or the button to verify your phone number.

Q: What if I'm still not receiving the verification code?
A: Check your phone's settings, reconnect your phone to the internet, and try again.

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Click on the "Forgot password" option on the home screen or the security page in your Telegram app. Enter your phone number to receive a password reset code.

Q: How can I report a problem?
A: You can report problems through the customer support option in the main menu.
