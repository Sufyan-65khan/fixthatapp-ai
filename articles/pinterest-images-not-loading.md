# Troubleshooting Guide: Pinterest Images Not Loading

## Problem Description
Pinterest may fail to load images, showing blank spaces, broken image icons, or endlessly loading placeholders. This affects your ability to browse pins, boards, and discover new content.

## Possible Causes
1. **Slow internet connection**: Pinterest loads many high-resolution images that require good bandwidth.
2. **App cache overloaded**: Too much cached data can slow down or break image loading.
3. **Outdated app version**: Older versions may have bugs affecting image rendering.
4. **Browser extensions interfering**: Ad blockers or privacy extensions can block Pinterest images.
5. **Device storage full**: Low storage can prevent the app from caching images properly.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Your Internet Connection
- Test your connection by loading other image-heavy websites.
- Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to see if one works better.
- Try restarting your router if on Wi-Fi.

### Fix 2: Clear Pinterest Cache
- On mobile: Go to Pinterest Settings > Privacy and Data > Clear Cache.
- On browser: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload Pinterest.
- This removes stored temporary data that may be corrupted.

### Fix 3: Disable Browser Extensions
- If using Pinterest in a browser, disable ad blockers and privacy extensions temporarily.
- Try loading Pinterest in an incognito or private browsing window.
- If images load in incognito mode, one of your extensions is causing the issue.

### Fix 4: Update the Pinterest App
- Go to the App Store or Google Play Store.
- Search for Pinterest and tap Update if available.
- Restart the app after updating.

### Fix 5: Free Up Device Storage
- Check your available storage in device settings.
- Delete unused apps, old photos, or videos to free up space.
- At least 500MB of free space is recommended for smooth app performance.

### Fix 6: Reinstall Pinterest
- Uninstall Pinterest from your device.
- Restart your device.
- Reinstall from the App Store or Google Play Store.
- Log back in and check if images load properly.

## When to Contact Support
If images still won't load after trying all fixes, visit help.pinterest.com to report the issue. Include screenshots of the problem and details about your device and app version.

## FAQ
Q: Why do some Pinterest images load but others don't?
A: This could indicate that specific images were removed by the original uploader or that they're hosted on servers experiencing issues. It could also be a partial cache problem.

Q: Can I use Pinterest offline?
A: Pinterest doesn't have a full offline mode, but recently viewed pins may be available from cache. You need an internet connection to browse and discover new content.

Q: Why are Pinterest images blurry?
A: Pinterest may load lower-quality versions of images on slow connections. Check your internet speed and make sure Data Saver mode is turned off in Pinterest settings.

Q: Does Pinterest use a lot of data?
A: Yes, Pinterest is image-heavy and can use significant data. Use Wi-Fi when possible, and enable Data Saver mode in the app settings to reduce data usage.
