# Troubleshooting Guide: Midjourney Bot Not Responding

## Problem Description
The Midjourney bot on Discord may not respond to your prompts, show error messages, or fail to generate images. Your /imagine commands may go unanswered, or the bot may appear offline.

## Possible Causes
1. **Midjourney server issues**: The service may be experiencing high load or downtime.
2. **Discord connection problems**: Your Discord client may have connectivity issues.
3. **Subscription expired**: Your Midjourney subscription may have ended or your GPU minutes may be used up.
4. **Bot banned in channel**: The Midjourney bot may not have permissions in the channel you're using.
5. **Command syntax errors**: Incorrect prompt formatting can cause the bot to not respond.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Midjourney Status
- Visit status.midjourney.com for service status updates.
- Check the #status channel in the official Midjourney Discord server.
- If there's an outage, wait for the team to resolve it.

### Fix 2: Verify Your Subscription
- Go to midjourney.com/account to check your subscription status.
- Verify you have remaining GPU minutes (Fast or Relax mode).
- Renew your subscription if it has expired.

### Fix 3: Try a Different Channel
- Try using the bot in one of the official Midjourney newbie channels.
- If using a private server, make sure the Midjourney bot is properly added and has permissions.
- Re-invite the bot if necessary from midjourney.com.

### Fix 4: Restart Discord
- Close Discord completely and reopen it.
- On desktop: Right-click the Discord icon in the system tray and click Quit, then reopen.
- On mobile: Force close the app and restart it.

### Fix 5: Use the Midjourney Website
- Go to midjourney.com and log in.
- You can generate images directly on the website without using Discord.
- This bypasses any Discord-specific issues.

### Fix 6: Check Your Prompt
- Make sure you're using the correct command: /imagine prompt: [your description]
- Avoid banned words or content that violates Midjourney's terms of service.
- Keep prompts clear and specific for best results.

## When to Contact Support
If the bot still doesn't respond after trying all fixes, submit a support ticket through the Midjourney website or ask for help in the #support channel on the Midjourney Discord server.

## FAQ
Q: Why does Midjourney say "Job queued"?
A: Your image generation request is waiting in line. During busy periods, there may be a queue. Paid plans with Fast mode get priority processing.

Q: Can I use Midjourney for free?
A: Midjourney occasionally offers free trials, but generally requires a paid subscription starting at $10/month.

Q: Why is my Midjourney image generation slow?
A: If you've used all your Fast GPU minutes, you'll be switched to Relax mode which is slower. Upgrade your plan or wait for your monthly minutes to reset.

Q: How do I add Midjourney bot to my own Discord server?
A: Go to midjourney.com, log in, and use the bot invitation link to add it to your server. Make sure the bot has appropriate permissions in the channels you want to use it in.
