# Troubleshooting Guide: FaceTime Not Connecting

## Problem Description
FaceTime calls may fail to connect, drop immediately after answering, or show a "FaceTime Unavailable" error. Video calls may also show a black screen or have no audio.

## Possible Causes
1. **Internet connection**: FaceTime requires a stable Wi-Fi or cellular data connection.
2. **FaceTime disabled**: The service may be turned off in your settings.
3. **Apple server issues**: Apple's FaceTime servers may be down.
4. **Regional restrictions**: FaceTime is not available in all countries.
5. **Date and time incorrect**: Wrong date/time settings can prevent FaceTime from working.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Internet Connection
- Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data.
- FaceTime video calls need at least 1 Mbps bandwidth.
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and cellular data.

### Fix 2: Enable FaceTime
- Go to Settings > FaceTime > Toggle it On.
- Make sure your phone number and Apple ID are listed under "You Can Be Reached By FaceTime At".
- If already on, toggle it off, wait 30 seconds, and toggle back on.

### Fix 3: Check Date and Time
- Go to Settings > General > Date & Time.
- Enable "Set Automatically".
- Incorrect date/time can prevent Apple services from authenticating.

### Fix 4: Restart Your Device
- Power off your iPhone, iPad, or Mac completely.
- Wait 30 seconds and turn it back on.
- Try making a FaceTime call again.

### Fix 5: Reset Network Settings
- Go to Settings > General > Transfer or Reset iPhone > Reset > Reset Network Settings.
- Reconnect to Wi-Fi after the reset.
- Try FaceTime again.

### Fix 6: Check Apple System Status
- Visit apple.com/support/systemstatus.
- Check if FaceTime shows any issues.
- If there's an outage, wait for Apple to fix it.

## When to Contact Support
If FaceTime still won't work after all fixes, contact Apple Support at support.apple.com or visit an Apple Store.

## FAQ
Q: Can I use FaceTime with Android users?
A: Yes, you can create FaceTime links that Android users can join through a web browser. However, Android users cannot initiate FaceTime calls.

Q: Why is FaceTime not available in my country?
A: FaceTime is restricted in some countries due to local regulations. Using a VPN may help but isn't guaranteed. Check Apple's feature availability page for your region.

Q: How many people can be on a FaceTime call?
A: FaceTime supports up to 32 participants in a group call.

Q: Why is there no sound on my FaceTime call?
A: Check your volume, make sure you're not on mute, and verify that FaceTime has microphone access in Settings > Privacy > Microphone.
