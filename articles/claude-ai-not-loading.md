# Troubleshooting Guide: Claude AI Not Loading

## Problem Description
Claude AI by Anthropic may fail to load, show error messages, give incomplete responses, or become unresponsive. You might see "Something went wrong" errors or experience the page not loading at all.

## Possible Causes
1. **Server overload**: High demand periods can cause Claude to become slow or unavailable.
2. **Browser issues**: Cache, cookies, or extensions may interfere with Claude's interface.
3. **Usage limits reached**: Free tier users may hit daily or hourly message limits.
4. **Internet connection**: A weak connection can cause timeouts and loading failures.
5. **Regional access**: Claude may not be available in all regions.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Anthropic Status
- Visit status.anthropic.com to check for ongoing issues.
- If there's an outage, wait for Anthropic to resolve it.
- Outages are usually resolved within a few hours.

### Fix 2: Refresh and Clear Cache
- Hard refresh the page with Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).
- Clear browser cache and cookies for claude.ai.
- Try accessing Claude in an incognito or private browsing window.

### Fix 3: Check Usage Limits
- Free tier users have limited messages per day.
- If you've hit your limit, wait for it to reset or upgrade to Claude Pro.
- Claude Pro ($20/month) offers significantly higher usage limits.

### Fix 4: Try a Different Browser
- If using Chrome, try Firefox, Edge, or Safari.
- Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version.
- Disable ad blockers and privacy extensions temporarily.

### Fix 5: Check Your Internet Connection
- Test your connection by loading other websites.
- Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
- Restart your router if the connection is unstable.

### Fix 6: Use the Claude Mobile App
- Download the Claude app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
- The mobile app may work when the website has issues.
- Sign in with your existing Anthropic account.

## When to Contact Support
If Claude continues to not work, use the help option within Claude or contact Anthropic support through their website. Pro subscribers may have access to priority support.

## FAQ
Q: Is Claude AI free?
A: Yes, Claude has a free tier with daily usage limits. Claude Pro ($20/month) and Team plans offer higher limits and additional features.

Q: Why does Claude say I've reached my limit?
A: Free users have a daily message limit that resets every few hours. During peak times, limits may be lower. Upgrading to Pro increases your limits significantly.

Q: What's the difference between Claude and ChatGPT?
A: Both are AI assistants but from different companies (Anthropic and OpenAI). They have different strengths — Claude is known for longer context handling and safety, while ChatGPT has a larger plugin ecosystem.

Q: Can I use Claude for coding?
A: Yes, Claude is excellent at coding tasks including writing, debugging, and explaining code in various programming languages.
