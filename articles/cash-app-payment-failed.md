# Troubleshooting Guide: Cash App Payment Failed

## Problem Description
Cash App may decline your payment, showing errors like "This payment was declined", "Cash App transfer failed", or simply failing without an explanation. This can happen when sending money to friends, paying merchants, or using your Cash Card.

## Possible Causes
1. **Insufficient funds**: Your Cash App balance or linked bank account may not have enough money.
2. **Card expired or invalid**: Your linked debit card or bank account may have issues.
3. **Unverified account**: Unverified accounts have lower sending limits.
4. **Security flag**: Cash App may flag the transaction as potentially fraudulent.
5. **Network issues**: A poor internet connection can cause payment failures.
6. **Recipient issues**: The recipient's account may not be able to receive payments.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Your Balance
- Open Cash App and check your available balance.
- If sending from a bank account, make sure it has sufficient funds.
- Check if any pending transactions are holding your funds.

### Fix 2: Verify Your Account
- Tap your profile icon > Personal > Verify Identity.
- Provide your full name, date of birth, and SSN (last 4 digits).
- Verified accounts can send up to $7,500 per week vs $250 for unverified.

### Fix 3: Update Payment Method
- Go to your Cash App profile > Linked Banks.
- Remove and re-add your debit card or bank account.
- Make sure the card isn't expired and has the correct details.

### Fix 4: Check Internet Connection
- Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
- Make sure you have a stable connection.
- Try the payment again with a better connection.

### Fix 5: Update Cash App
- Go to the App Store or Google Play Store.
- Update Cash App to the latest version.
- Restart the app and retry the payment.

### Fix 6: Try a Smaller Amount
- If sending a large amount, try sending a smaller amount first.
- Unverified accounts have daily and weekly limits.
- Large transactions may trigger security checks.

## When to Contact Support
If payments keep failing, contact Cash App support through the app: Profile > Support > Something Else > Contact Support. You can also reach them on Twitter @CashSupport.

## FAQ
Q: Why does Cash App say "payment failed for my protection"?
A: Cash App's security system flagged the transaction. This can happen with unusual amounts, new recipients, or suspicious activity. Verify your identity and try again.

Q: Can I cancel a failed Cash App payment?
A: Failed payments are automatically returned to your balance. If the payment is pending, you can cancel it by tapping the payment in your activity feed.

Q: How long does it take for a failed payment to be refunded?
A: Failed payments are usually returned instantly. Bank transfers may take 1-3 business days to reflect back in your bank account.

Q: Is there a daily limit on Cash App?
A: Unverified accounts can send up to $250 per week. Verified accounts can send up to $7,500 per week. Cash Card purchases have a daily limit of $7,000.
