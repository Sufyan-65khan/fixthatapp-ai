# Troubleshooting Guide: Twitter (X) App Crashing

## Problem Description
The Twitter (now X) app may crash unexpectedly when opening the app, scrolling through the feed, or trying to post a tweet. The app might close immediately on launch or freeze before crashing.

## Possible Causes
1. **Outdated app version**: Running an old version of the app may cause stability issues.
2. **Corrupted cache**: Accumulated temporary data can cause the app to malfunction.
3. **Operating system incompatibility**: Your phone's OS may need updating to support the latest version of X.
4. **Low device memory**: If your phone is running low on RAM, apps may crash frequently.
5. **Server-side issues**: Twitter/X servers may be experiencing problems.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Force Close and Restart the App
- On Android: Go to Settings > Apps > X (Twitter) > Force Stop.
- On iPhone: Swipe up from the bottom and swipe the X app away.
- Wait a few seconds and reopen the app.

### Fix 2: Clear App Cache
- On Android: Settings > Apps > X (Twitter) > Storage > Clear Cache.
- On iPhone: You'll need to uninstall and reinstall the app to clear the cache.
- This removes temporary files without deleting your account data.

### Fix 3: Update the App
- Open the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android).
- Search for "X" or "Twitter" and tap Update if available.
- App updates often include bug fixes for crashing issues.

### Fix 4: Free Up Device Memory
- Close other apps running in the background.
- Delete apps or files you no longer need.
- Restart your phone to free up RAM.

### Fix 5: Update Your Operating System
- On Android: Settings > System > Software Update.
- On iPhone: Settings > General > Software Update.
- Install any available updates and try opening the app again.

### Fix 6: Reinstall the App
- Uninstall X (Twitter) from your device.
- Restart your phone.
- Reinstall from the App Store or Google Play Store.
- Log back in and check if the crashing issue is resolved.

## When to Contact Support
If the app continues to crash after trying all fixes, report the issue at help.twitter.com or through the app (if accessible): Settings > Help Center. Include your device model, OS version, and app version.

## FAQ
Q: Why does the X app crash only when I open a specific feature?
A: This could be a bug in that specific feature. Make sure you're running the latest version. If the issue persists, report it to X support with details about which feature causes the crash.

Q: Can I use Twitter/X on a web browser instead?
A: Yes, you can access X at x.com or twitter.com from any mobile or desktop browser as a temporary workaround while troubleshooting the app.

Q: Will reinstalling the app delete my account?
A: No, your account and all your tweets are stored on X's servers. Reinstalling the app only removes the app from your device. Log back in and everything will be there.

Q: Why does X crash after an update?
A: Sometimes new updates have bugs. Check if other users are reporting the same issue on social media. If so, X will likely release a fix soon. You can also try rolling back to a previous version on Android using APK files.
