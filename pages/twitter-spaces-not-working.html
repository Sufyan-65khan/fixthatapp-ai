Troubleshooting Guide: Twitter/X Spaces Not Working

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Problem Description

Twitter/X Spaces may fail to load, have no audio, kick you out mid-conversation, show "Space not available" errors, or not let you join or create Spaces. This prevents you from participating in live audio conversations.

Possible Causes

App outdated: Old versions may have Spaces bugs.

Poor internet connection: Spaces require a stable connection for live audio.

Microphone permissions denied: The app can't access your microphone.

Account restrictions: New or restricted accounts may have limited Spaces access.

Server issues: X's servers may be experiencing problems.

Step-by-Step Fixes

Fix 1: Update X/Twitter App

• Go to App Store (iPhone) or Play Store (Android).

• Update to the latest version of X (formerly Twitter).

• Spaces features are frequently updated and may not work on old versions.

Fix 2: Check Internet Connection

• Spaces stream live audio and need a stable connection.

• Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to test.

• Move closer to your router or find a better signal area.

Fix 3: Grant Microphone Permission

• On iPhone: Settings > X > Microphone > toggle on.

• On Android: Settings > Apps > X > Permissions > Microphone > Allow.

• Without microphone access, you can listen but can't speak in Spaces.

Fix 4: Force Close and Reopen

• Swipe away the X app from recent apps.

• Wait a few seconds and reopen.

• Try joining the Space again.

Fix 5: Clear App Cache

• On Android: Settings > Apps > X > Storage > Clear Cache.

• On iPhone: uninstall and reinstall the app.

• Log back in and try Spaces again.

Fix 6: Check Account Eligibility

• Your account must be at least 30 days old to create Spaces.

• Private accounts can join but may have limited hosting features.

• Make sure your account isn't temporarily restricted.

When to Contact Support

If Spaces consistently don't work, report the issue through X: Settings > Help Center > Contact Us. Include details about the error.

FAQ

Q: Why can't I hear anything in a Space?

A: Check your phone volume, make sure you're not connected to Bluetooth audio, and try using headphones. Also check if the host has muted themselves.

Q: Can I join Spaces on desktop?

A: Yes, Spaces are available on x.com in a web browser. Some features may differ from the mobile app.

Q: Why was I kicked out of a Space?

A: The host may have ended the Space or removed you. If it happened suddenly, it could be a connection issue — try rejoining.

Q: Can I record a Space?

A: Hosts can record Spaces. As a listener, recording depends on the host's settings. Recorded Spaces are available for replay after they end.
