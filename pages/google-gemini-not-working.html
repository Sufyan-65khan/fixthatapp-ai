Troubleshooting Guide: Google Gemini Not Working

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Problem Description

Google Gemini (formerly Bard) may fail to load, produce errors, give incomplete responses, or become unresponsive. You might see messages like "Something went wrong" or experience long loading times without getting a response.

Possible Causes

Google server issues: Gemini's servers may be under heavy load or experiencing downtime.

Region restrictions: Gemini may not be available in your country or region.

Google account issues: Your account may need verification or may have restrictions.

Browser compatibility: Some browsers may not fully support Gemini's interface.

VPN interference: VPN connections can cause access issues with Google services.

Step-by-Step Fixes

Fix 1: Check Google's Service Status

• Visit workspace.google.com/status to see if Google services are experiencing issues.

• Check downdetector.com for Google Gemini reports.

• If there's an outage, wait for Google to fix it.

Fix 2: Check Regional Availability

• Gemini is not available in all countries. Check Google's documentation for supported regions.

• If you're using a VPN, try disconnecting it — your VPN location may be in an unsupported region.

• If Gemini isn't available in your region, you may need to wait for Google to expand availability.

Fix 3: Clear Browser Data

• Clear cache and cookies specifically for google.com.

• Try accessing Gemini in an incognito or private window.

• Disable browser extensions that might interfere.

Fix 4: Switch Google Accounts

• If you have multiple Google accounts, try switching to a different one.

• Personal Google accounts may have different access levels than Workspace accounts.

• Make sure your account is in good standing and email is verified.

Fix 5: Try the Gemini Mobile App

• Download the Google Gemini app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

• The mobile app may work when the web version has issues.

• Sign in with the same Google account.

Fix 6: Update Your Browser

• Make sure you're using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.

• Gemini works best in Google Chrome.

• Clear your browser cache after updating.

When to Contact Support

If Gemini continues to have issues, use the feedback button within Gemini to report the problem. You can also visit support.google.com for help with Google services.

FAQ

Q: Is Google Gemini free?

A: Yes, Gemini has a free tier. Gemini Advanced (with the most capable models) is available through Google One AI Premium for $19.99/month.

Q: What happened to Google Bard?

A: Google Bard was rebranded to Google Gemini in February 2024. If you have old bookmarks for bard.google.com, they should redirect to gemini.google.com.

Q: Can I use Gemini without a Google account?

A: No, you need a Google account to use Gemini. Sign up for a free Google account if you don't have one.

Q: Why does Gemini give different answers each time?

A: AI models generate responses based on probabilities, so responses can vary. If you need consistent answers, try being more specific in your prompts.
