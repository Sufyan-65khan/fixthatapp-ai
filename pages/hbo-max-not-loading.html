Troubleshooting Guide: HBO Max Not Loading

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Problem Description

HBO Max (now Max) may fail to load content, show error codes, buffer constantly, crash on startup, or display a blank screen. You may also encounter login issues or problems with specific shows and movies.

Possible Causes

App outdated: Old app versions may not work properly after platform updates.

Internet connection slow: Streaming requires a stable internet connection.

Device incompatible: Older devices may lose support for the app.

Cache corrupted: Stored app data may cause loading issues.

Server outage: HBO Max/Max servers may be experiencing problems.

Subscription expired: Your billing may have failed.

Step-by-Step Fixes

Fix 1: Check Internet Connection

• Run a speed test — HBO Max needs at least 5 Mbps for HD streaming.

• Try loading other apps or websites to confirm your internet works.

• Restart your router if speeds are slow.

Fix 2: Force Close and Reopen

• Close the Max app completely (swipe away from recent apps).

• Wait a few seconds and reopen it.

• This clears temporary glitches that cause loading failures.

Fix 3: Update the App

• Go to your device's app store and check for Max/HBO Max updates.

• The app was rebranded from "HBO Max" to "Max" — make sure you have the correct app.

• Update your streaming device's firmware too.

Fix 4: Clear Cache

• On Android/Fire TV: Settings > Apps > Max > Storage > Clear Cache.

• On smart TVs: uninstall and reinstall the app.

• On iPhone: delete and reinstall the app.

Fix 5: Check Subscription Status

• Go to your account at max.com.

• Verify your subscription is active and payment method is valid.

• Update your payment method if the card has expired.

Fix 6: Try a Different Device

• If Max doesn't work on your TV, try your phone, tablet, or computer.

• If it works elsewhere, the issue is with the original device.

• Check if your device is still supported — very old smart TVs may lose app support.

When to Contact Support

If Max still won't load, contact support at help.max.com. For billing issues, check your subscription provider (direct, Apple, Google, Amazon, etc.).

FAQ

Q: What happened to HBO Max?

A: HBO Max was rebranded to "Max" in 2023. If you have the old HBO Max app, update it or download the new Max app.

Q: Why does Max keep crashing on my smart TV?

A: Your TV may be running low on memory. Try uninstalling unused apps, clearing the Max cache, or factory resetting the TV.

Q: Can I watch Max on multiple devices?

A: Yes, depending on your plan. The basic plan allows 2 simultaneous streams, while the Ultimate plan allows 4.

Q: Why is video quality bad on Max?

A: Check your internet speed and make sure your plan supports HD or 4K. Also check the quality settings within the app.
