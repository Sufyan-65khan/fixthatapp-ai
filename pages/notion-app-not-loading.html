Troubleshooting Guide: Notion App Not Loading

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Problem Description

Notion may fail to load, showing a blank white screen, spinning loader, or "Something went wrong" error. Pages may not render properly, or the app may crash on startup.

Possible Causes

Internet connection: Notion requires internet for syncing and loading.

Browser cache issues: Corrupted cache can prevent Notion from rendering.

Extension conflicts: Browser extensions may interfere with Notion.

Server outage: Notion's servers may be experiencing downtime.

Large workspace: Very large workspaces with many pages can slow down loading.

Step-by-Step Fixes

Fix 1: Check Notion Status

• Visit status.notion.so to check for ongoing issues.

• If there's an outage, wait for Notion to resolve it.

Fix 2: Hard Refresh

• Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) for a hard refresh.

• This bypasses the cache and loads fresh content.

Fix 3: Clear Browser Cache

• Clear cache and cookies specifically for notion.so.

• Try Notion in an incognito window to rule out extension conflicts.

• If it works in incognito, disable extensions one by one to find the culprit.

Fix 4: Use the Desktop App

• Download Notion's desktop app from notion.so/download.

• The desktop app can be more stable than the browser version.

• Log in with the same account.

Fix 5: Check Internet Connection

• Test your connection by browsing other sites.

• Notion needs a stable connection for real-time syncing.

• Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile hotspot.

Fix 6: Clear Notion Desktop Cache

• Close Notion completely.

• On Windows: Delete contents of %AppData%\Notion.

• On Mac: Delete ~/Library/Application Support/Notion.

• Reopen Notion and log in again.

When to Contact Support

If Notion still doesn't load, contact support at notion.so/help or email team@notion.so.

FAQ

Q: Can I use Notion offline?

A: The desktop and mobile apps have limited offline support for recently accessed pages. Changes sync when you reconnect.

Q: Will clearing cache delete my Notion data?

A: No, all your Notion data is stored in the cloud. Clearing local cache only removes temporary files.

Q: Why is Notion slow with large databases?

A: Large databases with thousands of entries can slow Notion down. Use filters and views to limit displayed data, or split large databases into smaller ones.

Q: Is Notion free?

A: Yes, Notion has a free plan with unlimited pages. The Plus plan ($10/month) adds unlimited file uploads and more features.
