Troubleshooting Guide: DoorDash App Not Working

Problem Description

The DoorDash app may crash, fail to load restaurants, not process orders, or show error messages when trying to place or track a delivery. This can leave you unable to order food when you need it.

Possible Causes

App outdated: An older version of DoorDash may have bugs.

Internet connection: Poor connectivity can prevent the app from loading.

Location services disabled: DoorDash needs your location to show nearby restaurants.

Server issues: DoorDash's servers may be experiencing problems.

Account issues: Payment method problems or account restrictions.

Cache corruption: Corrupted app data can cause various errors.

Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Your Internet Connection

• Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data.

• Test your connection by opening a website.

• Restart your router if using Wi-Fi.

### Fix 2: Enable Location Services

• On iPhone: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > DoorDash > While Using the App.

• On Android: Settings > Apps > DoorDash > Permissions > Location > Allow.

• DoorDash needs location access to show restaurants near you.

### Fix 3: Update the App

• Go to the App Store or Google Play Store.

• Search for DoorDash and tap Update if available.

• Restart the app after updating.

### Fix 4: Clear App Cache

• On Android: Settings > Apps > DoorDash > Storage > Clear Cache.

• On iPhone: Uninstall and reinstall the app.

• This removes corrupted temporary data.

### Fix 5: Check Payment Method

• Open DoorDash > Account > Payment Methods.

• Verify your card is valid and not expired.

• Try adding a different payment method.

### Fix 6: Reinstall DoorDash

• Uninstall DoorDash from your device.

• Restart your phone.

• Reinstall from the App Store or Google Play Store.

• Log back in and try placing an order.

When to Contact Support

If the app still doesn't work, contact DoorDash support through the app's Help section or visit help.doordash.com. For active order issues, use the live chat for faster assistance.

FAQ

Q: Why can't I see any restaurants on DoorDash?

A: Check your location settings and make sure DoorDash has location access. Also verify you're in an area served by DoorDash.

Q: Why was my DoorDash order cancelled?

A: Orders can be cancelled if the restaurant is too busy, there are no available Dashers, or there's a payment issue. You'll receive a full refund for cancelled orders.

Q: Can I use DoorDash on a computer?

A: Yes, visit doordash.com to order from a web browser if the app isn't working.

Q: Why does DoorDash charge me more than the menu price?

A: DoorDash adds delivery fees, service fees, and sometimes small order fees. Menu prices may also be slightly higher than in-restaurant prices. Check the order summary before confirming.
