Troubleshooting Guide: iPhone Camera Not Working

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Problem Description

Your iPhone camera may show a black screen, freeze, fail to switch between front and rear cameras, or refuse to open. The Camera app might crash, or photos may appear blurry or overexposed.

Possible Causes

Software glitch: A temporary bug in iOS or the Camera app.

Case blocking the lens: Your phone case may be covering the camera.

Storage full: No free storage means no space to save new photos.

Third-party app conflict: Another app may be using the camera.

Hardware damage: Physical damage to the camera module.

Step-by-Step Fixes

Fix 1: Force Close the Camera App

• Swipe up from the bottom and close the Camera app.

• Wait a few seconds and reopen it.

• This resolves most temporary glitches.

Fix 2: Remove Your Case

• Some cases can block the camera lens or flash.

• Remove your case and test the camera.

• If it works without the case, get a case that doesn't obstruct the camera.

Fix 3: Clean the Lens

• Gently wipe the camera lens with a soft, lint-free cloth.

• Remove any fingerprints, dust, or debris.

• A dirty lens causes blurry or hazy photos.

Fix 4: Check Storage Space

• Go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage.

• If storage is almost full, delete old photos, apps, or videos.

• The camera can't save new photos without free space.

Fix 5: Restart Your iPhone

• Power off your iPhone completely.

• Wait 30 seconds and turn it back on.

• Open the Camera app and test.

Fix 6: Update iOS

• Go to Settings > General > Software Update.

• Install any available updates.

• Camera bugs are often fixed in iOS updates.

Fix 7: Reset All Settings

• Go to Settings > General > Transfer or Reset iPhone > Reset > Reset All Settings.

• This doesn't delete your data but resets all system settings.

When to Contact Support

If the camera still doesn't work, especially after a drop or water exposure, there may be hardware damage. Visit Apple Support or an Apple Store for diagnosis.

FAQ

Q: Why does my iPhone camera show a black screen?

A: Force close the Camera app, restart your phone, and make sure no case or accessory is covering the lens. If it persists, it may be a hardware issue.

Q: Why is my iPhone camera blurry?

A: Clean the lens first. If still blurry, check if your case is interfering, restart the phone, or try resetting camera settings.

Q: Can I use a third-party camera app if the default one doesn't work?

A: Yes, try apps like Halide or ProCamera. If third-party apps also don't work, the issue is hardware-related.

Q: Why does only the front/rear camera work?

A: One camera module may be damaged. Try switching cameras in the Camera app. If one doesn't work, contact Apple Support.
