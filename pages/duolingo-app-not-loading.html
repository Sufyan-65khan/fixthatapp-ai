Troubleshooting Guide: Duolingo App Not Loading

Problem Description

Duolingo may fail to load lessons, show a blank screen, get stuck on the loading screen, or crash when you try to start a lesson. This interrupts your language learning streak.

Possible Causes

Internet connection: Duolingo requires internet for most lessons.

Outdated app: Old versions may have bugs that prevent loading.

Cache corruption: Accumulated cache data can cause problems.

Server issues: Duolingo's servers may be experiencing downtime.

Device compatibility: Very old devices may struggle with the latest app.

Step-by-Step Fixes

### Fix 1: Check Internet Connection

• Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data.

• Try loading a website to confirm your connection works.

• Duolingo needs internet for lessons, leaderboards, and progress syncing.

### Fix 2: Force Close and Restart

• Close Duolingo completely from your recent apps.

• Wait a few seconds and reopen it.

• Try loading a lesson again.

### Fix 3: Clear App Cache

• On Android: Settings > Apps > Duolingo > Storage > Clear Cache.

• On iPhone: Uninstall and reinstall the app.

• This won't delete your progress — it's saved to your account.

### Fix 4: Update Duolingo

• Go to the App Store or Google Play Store.

• Update Duolingo to the latest version.

• Restart the app after updating.

### Fix 5: Log Out and Log Back In

• Go to Duolingo Settings > Sign Out.

• Close the app completely.

• Reopen and sign back in.

### Fix 6: Reinstall the App

• Uninstall Duolingo and restart your phone.

• Reinstall from your app store.

• Log in with your existing account — all progress is saved in the cloud.

When to Contact Support

If Duolingo still won't load, visit support.duolingo.com to report the issue. Include your device type, app version, and a description of the problem.

FAQ

Q: Will I lose my streak if Duolingo is down?

A: If there's a widespread outage, Duolingo sometimes grants streak repairs. You can also use a Streak Freeze to protect your streak.

Q: Can I use Duolingo offline?

A: With Duolingo Plus (Super), you can download lessons for offline use. Free users need an internet connection.

Q: Why is Duolingo so slow?

A: Close other apps, clear Duolingo's cache, and make sure you have a stable internet connection. If your device is old, the latest app version may be too resource-heavy.

Q: Is Duolingo free?

A: Yes, Duolingo has a free tier with ads and limited hearts. Super Duolingo (paid) removes ads and adds offline access and unlimited hearts.
